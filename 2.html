Instagram[a] is an American photo and short-form video sharing social networking service owned by Meta Platforms. It allows users to upload media that can be edited with filters, be organized by hashtags, and be associated with a location via geographical tagging.[7] Posts can be shared publicly or with preapproved followers. It supports 33 languages including English, Hindi, Spanish, French, Japanese, and Korean.[9]
